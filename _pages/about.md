Salvete omnes! I'm a Master's student at CU Boulder in the <a href='https://www.colorado.edu/linguistics/graduate-program/computational-linguistics-clasic-masters-degree'>CLASIC</a> program, looking for summer internships in NLP Engineering or machine learning positions more boradly.

I'm interested in making NLP tools accessible to the public, ranging from neurosymbolic systems for social scientists to AI helpers for students. While my current projects involve building NLP systems using PyTorch or TensorFlow, my previous career in sales and marketing keeps me focused on the end user.

My most recent position was Associate Manager at Johnson & Johnson where I worked in the Data Intelligence team. Extracting insights from volumes of messy sales data and designing dashboards to streamline communication informed my decision to pivot towards a data and engineering-centric career. Combined with my passion for language (fluent in English, Mandarin, and French), designing NLP systems comes naturally to me.

Thanks for visiting my page, feel free to check out my projects or contact me via email!
